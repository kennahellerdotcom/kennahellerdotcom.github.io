The Marlon Nash Drive

This drive organized by Homeless Health Care LA and the Nash/Ellenburg family is aimed to raise awareness to the overwhelming rates of overdose in the US and to destigmatize the use of narcotics.

The goal was to speak to the young-adult demographic - the same age Marlon was when he overdosed. HHCLA / Marlon's family believe that more people can be saved with information that prevents overdosing.
